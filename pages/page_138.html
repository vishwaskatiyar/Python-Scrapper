Nutrient deficiencies occur when the body does not get enough essential vitamins, minerals, or other nutrients needed for proper function. Iron deficiency can lead to fatigue, weakness, and anemia. It is crucial for the production of hemoglobin, which carries oxygen in the blood.

Vitamin D is important for bone health and immune function. Deficiency can result in bone pain, muscle weakness, and increased susceptibility to infections.
